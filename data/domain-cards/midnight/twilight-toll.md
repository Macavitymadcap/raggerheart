## TWILIGHT TOLL

**Level 9 Midnight Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Choose a target within Far range. When you succeed on an action roll against
them that doesn't result in making a damage roll, place a token on this card.
When you deal damage to this target, spend any number of tokens to add a **d12**
for each token spent to your damage roll. You can only hold Twilight Toll on one
creature at a time.

When you choose a new target or take a rest, clear all unspent tokens.
