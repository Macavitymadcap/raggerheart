## BOOST

**Level 4 Bone Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

**Mark a Stress** to boost off a willing ally within Close range, fling yourself
into the air, and perform an aerial attack against a target within Far range.
You have advantage on the attack, add a **d10** to the damage roll, and end your
move within Melee range of the target.
