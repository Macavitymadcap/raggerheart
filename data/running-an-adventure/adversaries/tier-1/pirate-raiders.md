### PIRATE RAIDERS

***Tier 1 Horde (1/HP)***

_Seafaring scoundrels moving in a ravaging pack._

**Motives & Tactics:** Gang up, plunder, raid

**Difficulty:** 12 | **Thresholds:** 5/11 | **HP:** 4 | **Stress:** 3

**ATK:** +1 | **Cutlass:** Melee | 1d8+2 phy

**Experience:** Sailor +3

**FEATURES**

***Horde (1d4+1) - Passive:*** When the Raiders have marked half or more of
their HP, their standard attack deals **1d4+1** physical damage instead.

***Swashbuckler - Passive:*** When the Raiders mark 2 or fewer HP from an attack
within Melee range, the attacker must mark a Stress.
