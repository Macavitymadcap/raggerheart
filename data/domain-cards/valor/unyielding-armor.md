## UNYIELDING ARMOR

**Level 10 Valor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When you would mark an Armor Slot, roll a number of **d6s** equal to your
Proficiency. If any roll a 6, reduce the severity by one threshold without
marking an Armor Slot.
