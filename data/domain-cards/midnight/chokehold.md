## CHOKEHOLD

**Level 3 Midnight Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When you position yourself behind a creature who's about your size, you can
**mark a Stress** to pull them into a chokehold, making them temporarily
_Vulnerable_.

When a creature attacks a target who is _Vulnerable_ in this way, they deal an
extra **2d6** damage.
