## ECLIPSE

**Level 10 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 2**

Make a **Spellcast Roll (16)**. Once per long rest on a success, plunge the
entire area within Far range into complete darkness only you and your allies can
see through. Attack rolls have disadvantage when targeting you or an ally within
this shadow.

Additionally, when you or an ally succeeds with Hope against an adversary within
this shadow, the target must mark a Stress.

This spell lasts until the GM spends a Fear on their turn to clear this effect
or you take Severe damage.
