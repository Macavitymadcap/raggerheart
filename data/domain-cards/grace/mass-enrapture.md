## MASS ENRAPTURE

**Level 8 Grace Spell**

**Recall Cost: 3**

Make a **Spellcast Roll** against all targets within Far range. Targets you
succeed against become temporarily _Enraptured_. While _Enraptured_, a target's
attention is fixed on you, narrowing their field of view and drowning out any
sound but your voice. **Mark a Stress** to force all _Enraptured_ targets to
mark a Stress, ending this spell.
