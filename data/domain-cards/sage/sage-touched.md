## SAGE-TOUCHED

**Level 7 Sage Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

When 4 or more of the domain cards in your loadout are from the Sage domain,
gain the following benefits:

- While you're in a natural environment, you gain a +2 bonus to your Spellcast
  Rolls. 
- Once per rest, you can double your Agility or Instinct when making a
  roll that uses that trait. You must choose to do this before you roll.
