## LEAD BY EXAMPLE

**Level 9 Valor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 3**

When you deal damage to an adversary, you can **mark a Stress** and describe how
you encourage your allies. The next PC to make an attack against that adversary
can clear a Stress or gain a Hope.
