### ZOMBIE PACK

***Tier 1 Horde (1/HP)***

_A group of shambling corpses instinctively moving together._

**Motives & Tactics:** Consume flesh, hunger, maul

**Difficulty:** 8 | **Thresholds:** 6/12 | **HP:** 6 | **Stress:** 3

**ATK:** -1 | **Bite:** Melee | 1d10+2 phy

**FEATURES**

***Horde (1d4+2) - Passive:*** When the Zombies have marked half or more of
their HP, their standard attack deals **1d4+2** physical damage instead.

***Overwhelm - Reaction:*** When the Zombies mark HP from an attack within Melee
range, you can **mark a Stress** to make a standard attack against the attacker.
