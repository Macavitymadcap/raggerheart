## PRESERVATION BLAST

**Level 4 Arcana Spell**

**Recall Cost: 2**

Make a **Spellcast Roll** against all targets within Melee range.
Targets you succeed against are forced back to Far range and
take **d8+3** magic damage using your Spellcast trait.
