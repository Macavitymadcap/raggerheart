## DISINTEGRATION WAVE

**Level 9 Codex Spell**

**Recall Cost: 4**

Make a **Spellcast Roll (18)**. Once per long rest on a success, the GM tells
you which adversaries within Far range have a Difficulty of 18 or lower. **Mark
a Stress** for each one you wish to hit with this spell. They are killed and
can't come back to life by any means.
