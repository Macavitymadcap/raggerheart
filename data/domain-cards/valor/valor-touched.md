## VALOR-TOUCHED

**Level 7 Valor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When 4 or more of the domain cards in your loadout are from the Valor domain,
gain the following benefits:

- +1 bonus to your Armor Score 
- When you mark 1 or more Hit Points without marking an Armor Slot, clear an 
  Armor Slot.
