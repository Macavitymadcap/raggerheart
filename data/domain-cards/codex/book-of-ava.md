## BOOK OF AVA

**Level 1 Codex Grimoire**

**Recall Cost: 2**

***Power Push:*** Make a **Spellcast Roll** against a target within Melee range.
On a success, they're knocked back to Far range and take **d10+2** magic damage
using your Proficiency.

***Tava's Armor:*** **Spend a Hope** to give a target you can touch a +1 bonus
to their Armor Score until their next rest or you cast Tava's Armor again.

***Ice Spike:*** Make a **Spellcast Roll (12)** to summon a large ice spike
within Far range. If you use it as a weapon, make the Spellcast Roll against the
target's Difficulty instead. On a success, deal **d6** physical damage using
your Proficiency.
