### ROTTED ZOMBIE

***Tier 1 Minion***

_A decaying corpse ambling toward their prey._

**Motives & Tactics:** Eat flesh, hunger, maul, surround

**Difficulty:** 8 | **Thresholds:** None | **HP:** 1 | **Stress:** 1

**ATK:** -3 | **Bite:** Melee | 2 phy

**FEATURES**

***Minion (3) - Passive:*** The Zombie is defeated when they take any damage.
For every 3 damage a PC deals to the Zombie, defeat an additional Minion within
range the attack would succeed against.

***Group Attack - Action:*** **Spend a Fear** to choose a target and spotlight
all Rotted Zombies within Close range of them. Those Minions move into Melee
range of the target and make one shared attack roll. On a success, they deal 2
physical damage each. Combine this damage.
