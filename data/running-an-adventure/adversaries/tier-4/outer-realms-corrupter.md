### OUTER REALMS CORRUPTER

***Tier 4 Support***

_A shifting, formless mass seemingly made of chromatic light._

**Motives & Tactics:** Confuse, distract, overwhelm

**Difficulty:** 19 | **Thresholds:** 27/47 | **HP:** 4 | **Stress:** 3

**ATK:** +7 | **Corroding Pseudopod:** Very Close | 4d8+5 mag

**FEATURES**

***Will-Shattering Touch - Passive:*** When a PC takes damage from the
Corrupter, they lose a Hope.

***Disgorge Reality Flotsam - Action:*** **Mark a Stress** to spew partially
digested portions of consumed realities at all targets within Close range.
Targets must succeed on a Knowledge Reaction Roll or mark 2 Stress.
