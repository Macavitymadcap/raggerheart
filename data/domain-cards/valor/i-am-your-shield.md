## I AM YOUR SHIELD

**Level 1 Valor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When an ally within Very Close range would take damage, you can **mark a
Stress** to stand in the way and make yourself the target of the attack instead.
When you take damage from this attack, you can mark any number of Armor Slots.
