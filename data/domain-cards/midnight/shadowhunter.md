## SHADOWHUNTER

**Level 8 Midnight Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

Your prowess is enhanced under the cover of shadow. While you're shrouded in low
light or darkness, you gain a +1 bonus to your Evasion and make attack rolls
with advantage.
