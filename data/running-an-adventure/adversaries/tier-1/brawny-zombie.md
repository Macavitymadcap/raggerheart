### BRAWNY ZOMBIE

***Tier 1 Bruiser***

_A large corpse, decay-bloated and angry._

**Motives & Tactics:** Crush, destroy, hail debris, slam

**Difficulty:** 10 | **Thresholds:** 8/15 | **HP:** 7 | **Stress:** 4

**ATK:** +2 | **Slam:** Very Close | 1d12+3 phy

**Experience:** Collateral Damage +2, Throw +4

**FEATURES**

***Slow - Passive:*** When you spotlight the Zombie and they don't have a token
on their stat block, they can't act yet. Place a token on their stat block and
describe what they're preparing to do. When you spotlight the Zombie and they
have a token on their stat block, clear the token and they can act.

***Rend Asunder - Action:*** Make a standard attack with advantage against a
target the Zombie has _Restrained_. On a success, the attack deals direct
damage.

***Rip and Tear - Reaction:*** When the Zombies makes a successful standard
attack, you can **mark a Stress** to temporarily _Restrain_ the target and force
them to mark 2 Stress.
