### ELECTRIC EELS

***Tier 2 Horde (1/HP)***

_A swarm of eels that encircle and electrocute._

**Motives & Tactics:** Avoid larger predators, shock prey, tear apart

**Difficulty:** 14 | **Thresholds:** 10/20 | **HP:** 5 | **Stress:** 3

**ATK:** +0 | **Shocking Bite:** Melee | 2d6+4 phy

**FEATURES**

***Horde (2d4+1) - Passive:*** When the Eels have marked half or more of their
HP, their standard attack deals **2d4+1** physical damage instead.

***Paralyzing Shock - Action:*** **Mark a Stress** to make a standard attack
against all targets within Very Close range. You gain a Fear for each target
that marks HP.
