### JAGGED KNIFE LIEUTENANT

***Tier 1 Leader***

_A seasoned bandit in quality leathers with a strong voice and cunning eyes._

**Motives & Tactics:** Bully, command, profit, reinforce

**Difficulty:** 13 | **Thresholds:** 7/14 | **HP:** 6 | **Stress:** 3

**ATK:** +2 | **Javelin:** Close | 1d8+3 phy

**Experience:** Local Knowledge +2

**FEATURES**

***Tactician - Action:*** When you spotlight the Lieutenant, **mark a Stress**
to also spotlight two allies within Close range.

***More Where That Came From - Action:*** Summon three Jagged Knife Lackeys, who
appear at Far range.

***Coup de Grace - Action:*** **Spend a Fear** to make an attack against a
_Vulnerable_ target within Close range. On a success, deal **2d6+12** physical
damage and the target must mark a Stress.

***Momentum - Reaction:*** When the Lieutenant makes a successful attack against
a PC, you gain a Fear.
