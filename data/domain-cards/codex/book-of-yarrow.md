## BOOK OF YARROW

**Level 10 Codex Grimoire**

**Recall Cost: 2**

***Timejammer:*** Make a **Spellcast Roll (18)**. On a success, time temporarily
slows to a halt for everyone within Far range except for you. It resumes the
next time you make an action roll that targets another creature.

***Magic Immunity:*** **Spend 5 Hope** to become immune to magic damage until
your next rest.
