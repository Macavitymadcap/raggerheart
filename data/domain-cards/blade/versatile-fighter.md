## VERSATILE FIGHTER

**Level 3 Blade Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

You can use a different character trait for an equipped weapon, rather than the
trait the weapon calls for.

When you deal damage, you can **mark a Stress** to use the maximum result of one
of your damage dice instead of rolling it.
