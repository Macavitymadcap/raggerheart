## CONJURED STEEDS

**Level 6 Sage Spell**

**Recall Cost: 0**

**Spend any number of Hope** to conjure that many magical steeds (such as
horses, camels, or elephants) that you and your allies can ride until your next
long rest or the steeds take any damage. The steeds double your land speed while
traveling and, when in danger, allow you to move within Far range without having
to roll. Creatures riding a steed gain a −2 penalty to attack rolls and a +2
bonus to damage rolls.
