### JAGGED KNIFE BANDIT

***Tier 1 Standard***

_A cunning criminal in a cloak bearing one of the gang's iconic knives._

**Motives & Tactics:** Escape, profit, steal, throw smoke

**Difficulty:** 12 | **Thresholds:** 8/14 | **HP:** 5 | **Stress:** 3

**ATK:** +1 | **Daggers:** Melee | 1d8+1 phy

**Experience:** Thief +2

**FEATURES**

***Climber - Passive:*** The Bandit climbs just as easily as they run.

***From Above - Passive:*** When the Bandit succeeds on a standard attack from
above a target, they deal **1d10+1** physical damage instead of their standard
damage.
