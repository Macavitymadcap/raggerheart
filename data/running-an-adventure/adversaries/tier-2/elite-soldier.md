### ELITE SOLDIER

***Tier 2 Standard***

_An armored squire or experienced commoner looking to advance._

**Motives & Tactics:** Gain glory, keep order, make alliances

**Difficulty:** 15 | **Thresholds:** 9/18 | **HP:** 4 | **Stress:** 3

**ATK:** +1 | **Spear:** Very Close | 2d8+4 phy

**FEATURES**

***Reinforce - Action:*** **Mark a Stress** to move into Melee range of an ally
and make a standard attack against a target within Very Close range. On a
success, deal **2d10+2** physical damage and the ally can clear a Stress.

***Vassal's Loyalty - Reaction:*** When the Soldier is within Very Close range
of a knight or other noble who would take damage, you can **mark a Stress** to
move into Melee range of them and take the damage instead.
