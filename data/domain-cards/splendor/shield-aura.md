## SHIELD AURA

**Level 8 Splendor Spell**

**Recall Cost: 2**

**Mark a Stress** to cast a protective aura on a target within Very Close range.
When the target marks an Armor Slot, they reduce the severity of the attack by
an additional threshold. If this spell causes a creature who would be damaged to
instead mark no Hit Points, the effect ends.

You can only hold Shield Aura on one creature at a time.
