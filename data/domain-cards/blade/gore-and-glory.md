## GORE AND GLORY

**Level 9 Blade Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

When you critically succeed on a weapon attack, gain an additional Hope or clear
an additional Stress.

Additionally, when you deal enough damage to defeat an enemy, gain a Hope or
clear a Stress.
