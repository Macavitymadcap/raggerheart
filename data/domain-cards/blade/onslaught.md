## ONSLAUGHT

**Level 10 Blade Ability**

**Recall Cost: 3**

When you successfully make an attack with your weapon, you never deal damage
beneath a target's Major damage threshold (the target always marks a minimum of
2 Hit Points).

Additionally, when a creature within your weapon's range deals damage to an ally
with an attack that doesn't include you, you can **mark a Stress** to force them
to make a Reaction Roll (15). On a failure, the target must mark a Hit Point.
