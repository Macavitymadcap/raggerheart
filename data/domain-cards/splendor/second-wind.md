## SECOND WIND

**Level 3 Splendor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

Once per rest, when you succeed on an attack against an adversary, you can clear
3 Stress or a Hit Point. On a success with Hope, you also clear 3 Stress or a
Hit Point on an ally within Close range of you.
