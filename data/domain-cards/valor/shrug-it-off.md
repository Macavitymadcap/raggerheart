## SHRUG IT OFF

**Level 7 Valor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When you would take damage, you can **mark a Stress** to reduce the severity of
the damage by one threshold. When you do, roll a **d6**. On a result of 3 or
lower, place this card in your vault.
