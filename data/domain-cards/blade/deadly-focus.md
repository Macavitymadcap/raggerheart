## DEADLY FOCUS

**Level 4 Blade Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

Once per rest, you can apply all your focus toward a target of your choice.
Until you attack another creature, you defeat the target, or the battle ends,
gain a +1 bonus to your Proficiency.
