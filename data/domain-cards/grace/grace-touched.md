## GRACE-TOUCHED

**Level 7 Grace Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

When 4 or more of the domain cards in your loadout are from the Grace domain,
gain the following benefits:

- You can **mark an Armor Slot** instead of marking a Stress. 
- When you would force a target to mark a number of Hit Points, you can choose 
  instead to force them to mark that number of Stress.
