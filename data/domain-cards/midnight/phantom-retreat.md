## PHANTOM RETREAT

**Level 5 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 2**

**Spend a Hope** to activate Phantom Retreat where you're currently standing.
**Spend another Hope** at any time before your next rest to disappear from where
you are and reappear where you were standing when you activated Phantom Retreat.
This spell ends after you reappear.
