## FRENZY

**Level 8 Blade Ability**

**Recall Cost: 3**

Once per long rest, you can go into a _Frenzy_ until there are no more
adversaries within sight.

While _Frenzied_, you can't use Armor Slots, and you gain a +10 bonus to your
damage rolls and a +8 bonus to your Severe damage threshold.
