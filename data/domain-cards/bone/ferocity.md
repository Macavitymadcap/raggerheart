## FEROCITY

**Level 2 Bone Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

When you cause an adversary to mark 1 or more Hit Points, you can **spend 2
Hope** to increase your Evasion by the number of Hit Points they marked. This
bonus lasts until after the next attack made against you.
