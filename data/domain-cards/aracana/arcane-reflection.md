## ARCANE REFELCTION

**Level 8 Arcana Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When you would take magic damage, you can s**pend any**
**number of Hope** to roll that many d6s. If any roll a 6, the
attack is reflected back to the caster, dealing the damage to
them instead.
