## DEFT MANEUVERS

**Level 1 Bone Ability**

**Recall Cost: 0**

Once per rest, **mark a Stress** to sprint anywhere within Far range without
making an Agility Roll to get there.

If you end this movement within Melee range of an adversary and immediately make
an attack against them, gain a +1 bonus to the attack roll.
