## TACTICIAN

**Level 3 Bone Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When you Help an Ally, they can spend a Hope to add one of your Experiences to
their roll alongside your advantage die.

When making a Tag Team Roll, you can roll a **d20** as your Hope Die.
