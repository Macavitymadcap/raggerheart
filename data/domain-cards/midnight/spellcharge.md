## SPELLCHARGE

**Level 8 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When you take magic damage, place tokens equal to the number of Hit Points you
marked on this card. You can store a number of tokens equal to your Spellcast
trait.

When you make a successful attack against a target, you can spend any number of
tokens to add a **d6** for each token spent to your damage roll.
