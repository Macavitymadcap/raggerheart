## UNCANNY DISGUISE

**Level 1 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 0**

When you have a few minutes to prepare, you can **mark a Stress** to don the
facade of any humanoid you can picture clearly in your mind. While disguised,
you have advantage on Presence Rolls to avoid scrutiny.

Place a number of tokens equal to your Spellcast trait on this card. When you
take an action while disguised, spend a token from this card. After the action
that spends the last token is resolved, the disguise drops.
