## VANISHING DODGE

**Level 7 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When an attack made against you that would deal physical damage fails, you can
**spend a Hope** to envelop yourself in shadow, becoming _Hidden_ and
teleporting to a point within Close range of the attacker. You remain _Hidden_
until the next time you make an action roll.
