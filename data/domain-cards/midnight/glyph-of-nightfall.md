## GLYPH OF NIGHTFALL

**Level 4 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Make a **Spellcast Roll** against a target within Very Close range. On a
success, **spend a Hope** to conjure a dark glyph upon their body that exposes
their weak points, temporarily reducing the target's Difficulty by a value equal
to your Knowledge (minimum 1).
