## TROUBLEMAKER

**Level 2 Grace Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

When you taunt or provoke a target within Far range, make a **Presence Roll**
against them. Once per rest on a success, roll a number of **d4s** equal to your
Proficiency. The target must mark Stress equal to the highest result rolled.
