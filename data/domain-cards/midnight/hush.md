## HUSH

**Level 5 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Make a **Spellcast Roll** against a target within Close range. On a success,
**spend a Hope** to conjure suppressive magic around the target that encompasses
everything within Very Close range of them and follows them as they move.

The target and anything within the area is _Silenced_ until the GM spends a Fear
on their turn to clear this condition, you cast Hush again, or you take Major
damage. While _Silenced_, they can't make noise and can't cast spells.
