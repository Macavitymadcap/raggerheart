## TRANSCENDENT UNION

**Level 10 Codex Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Once per long rest, **spend 5 Hope** to cast this spell on two or more willing
creatures. Until your next rest, when a creature connected by this union would
mark Stress or Hit Points, the connected creatures can choose who marks it.
