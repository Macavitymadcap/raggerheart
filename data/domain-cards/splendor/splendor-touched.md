## SPLENDOR-TOUCHED

**Level 7 Splendor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

When 4 or more of the domain cards in your loadout are from the Splendor domain,
gain the following benefits:

- +3 bonus to your Severe damage threshold 
- Once per long rest, when incoming damage would require you to mark a number of
  Hit Points, you can choose to mark that much Stress or spend that much Hope 
  instead.
