## VEIL OF NIGHT

**Level 3 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Make a **Spellcast Roll (13)**. On a success, you can create a temporary curtain
of darkness between two points within Far range. Only you can see through this
darkness. You're considered _Hidden_ to adversaries on the other side of the
veil, and you have advantage on attacks you make through the darkness. The veil
remains until you cast another spell.
