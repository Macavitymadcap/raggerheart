### MERCHANT

***Tier 1 Social***

_A finely dressed trader with a keen eye for financial gain._

**Motives & Tactics:** Buy low and sell high, create demand, inflate prices,
seek profit

**Difficulty:** 12 | **Thresholds:** 4/8 | **HP:** 3 | **Stress:** 3

**ATK:** -4 | **Club:** Melee | 1d4+1 phy

**Experience:** Shrewd Negotiator +3

**FEATURES**

***Preferential Treatment - Passive:*** A PC who succeeds on a Presence Roll
against the Merchant gains a discount on purchases. A PC who fails on a Presence
Roll against the Merchant must pay more and has disadvantage on future Presence
Rolls against the Merchant.

***The Runaround - Passive:*** When a PC rolls a 14 or lower on a Presence Roll
made against the Merchant, they must mark a Stress.
