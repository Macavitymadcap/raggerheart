## ENCORE

**Level 10 Grace Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When an ally within Close range deals damage to an adversary, you can make a
**Spellcast Roll** against that same target. On a success, you deal the same
damage to the target that your ally dealt. If your Spellcast Roll succeeds with
Fear, place this card in your vault.
