## OVERWHELMING AURA

**Level 9 Splendor Spell**

**Recall Cost: 2**

Make a **Spellcast Roll (15)** to magically empower your aura. On a success,
**spend 2 Hope** to make your Presence equal to your Spellcast trait until your
next long rest.

While this spell is active, an adversary must mark a Stress when they target you
with an attack.
