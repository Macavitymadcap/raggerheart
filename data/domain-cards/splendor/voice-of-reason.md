## VOICE OF REASON

**Level 3 Splendor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

You speak with an unmatched power and authority. You have advantage on action
rolls to de-escalate violent situations or convince someone to follow your lead.

Additionally, you're emboldened in moments of duress. When all of your Stress
slots are marked, you gain a +1 bonus to your Proficiency for damage rolls.
