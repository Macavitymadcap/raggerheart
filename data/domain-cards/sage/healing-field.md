## HEALING FIELD

**Level 4 Sage Spell**

**Recall Cost: 2**

Once per long rest, you can conjure a field of healing plants around you.
Everywhere within Close range of you bursts to life with vibrant nature,
allowing you and all allies in the area to clear a Hit Point.

**Spend 2 Hope** to allow you and all allies to clear 2 Hit Points instead.
