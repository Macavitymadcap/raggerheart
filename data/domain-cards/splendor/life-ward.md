## LIFE WARD

**Level 4 Splendor Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

**Spend 3 Hope** and choose an ally within Close range. They are marked with a
glowing sigil of protection. When this ally would make a death move, they clear
a Hit Point instead.

This effect ends when it saves the target from a death move, you cast Life Ward
on another target, or you take a long rest.
