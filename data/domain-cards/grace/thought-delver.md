## THOUGHT DELVER

**Level 5 Grace Spell**

**Recall Cost: 2**

You can peek into the minds of others. **Spend a Hope** to read the vague
surface thoughts of a target within Far range. Make a **Spellcast Roll** against
the target to delve for deeper, more hidden thoughts.

On a roll with Fear, the target might, at the GM's discretion, become aware that
you're reading their thoughts.
