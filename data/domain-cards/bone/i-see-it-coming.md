## I SEE IT COMING

**Level 1 Bone Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When you're targeted by an attack made from beyond Melee range, you can 
**mark a Stress** to roll a **d4** and gain a bonus to your Evasion equal to the 
result against the attack.
