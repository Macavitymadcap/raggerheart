## DIVINATION

**Level 4 Splendor Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Once per long rest, **spend 3 Hope** to reach out to the forces beyond and ask
one "yes or no" question about an event, person, place, or situation in the near
future. For a moment, the present falls away and you see the answer before you.
