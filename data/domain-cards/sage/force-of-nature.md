## FORCE OF NATURE

**Level 10 Sage Spell**

**Recall Cost: 2**

**Mark a Stress** to transform into a hulking nature spirit, gaining the
following benefits:

- When you succeed on an attack or Spellcast Roll, gain a +10 bonus to the
  damage roll. 
- When you deal enough damage to defeat a creature within Close range, you absorb
  them and clear an Armor Slot. 
- You can't be _Restrained_.

Before you make an action roll, you must **spend a Hope**. If you can't, you
revert to your normal form.
