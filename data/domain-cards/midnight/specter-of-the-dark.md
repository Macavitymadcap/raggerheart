## SPECTER OF THE DARK

**Level 10 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

**Mark a Stress** to become _Spectral_ until you make an action roll targeting
another creature. While _Spectral_, you're immune to physical damage and can
float and pass through solid objects. Other creatures can still see you while
you're in this form.
