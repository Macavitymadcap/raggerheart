### GIANT RECRUIT

***Tier 2 Minion***

_A giant fighter wearing borrowed armor._

**Motives & Tactics:** Batter, make a living, overwhelm, terrify

**Difficulty:** 13 | **Thresholds:** None | **HP:** 1 | **Stress:** 2

**ATK:** +1 | **Warhammer:** Very Close | 5 phy

**FEATURES**

***Minion (7) - Passive:*** The Recruit is defeated when they take any damage.
For every 7 damage a PC deals to the Recruit, defeat an additional Minion within
range the attack would succeed against.

***Group Attack - Action:*** **Spend a Fear** to choose a target and spotlight
all Giant Recruits within Close range of them. Those Minions move into Melee
range of the target and make one shared attack roll. On a success, they deal 5
physical damage each. Combine this damage.
