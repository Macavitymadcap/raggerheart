### JAGGED KNIFE LACKEY

***Tier 1 Minion***

_A thief with simple clothes and small daggers, eager to prove themselves._

**Motives & Tactics:** Escape, profit, throw smoke

**Difficulty:** 9 | **Thresholds:** None | **HP:** 1 | **Stress:** 1

**ATK:** -2 | **Daggers:** Melee | 2 phy

**Experience:** Thief +2

**FEATURES**

***Minion (3) - Passive:*** The Lackey is defeated when they take any damage.
For every 3 damage a PC deals to the Lackey, defeat an additional Minion within
range the attack would succeed against.

***Group Attack - Action:*** **Spend a Fear** to choose a target and spotlight
all Jagged Knife Lackeys within Close range of them. Those Minions move into
Melee range of the target and make one shared attack roll. On a success, they
deal 2 physical damage each. Combine this damage.
