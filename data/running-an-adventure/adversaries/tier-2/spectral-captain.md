### SPECTRAL CAPTAIN

***Tier 2 Leader***

_A ghostly commander leading their troops beyond death._

**Motives & Tactics:** Move through solid objects, rally troops, rehash old
battles

**Difficulty:** 16 | **Thresholds:** 13/26 | **HP:** 6 | **Stress:** 4

**ATK:** +3 | **Longbow:** Far | 2d10+3 phy

**Experience:** Ancient Knowledge +3

**FEATURES**

***Ghost - Passive:*** The Captain has resistance to physical damage. **Mark a
Stress** to move up to Close range through solid objects.

***Unending Battle - Action:*** **Spend 2 Fear** to return up to **1d4+1**
defeated Spectral allies to the battle at the points where they first appeared
(with no marked HP or Stress).

***Hold Fast - Reaction:*** When the Captain's Spectral allies are forced to
make a reaction roll, you can **mark a Stress** to give those allies a +2 bonus
to the roll.

***Momentum - Reaction:*** When the Captain makes a successful attack against a
PC, you gain a Fear.
