## TOWERING STALK

**Level 3 Sage Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Once per rest, you can conjure a thick, twisting stalk within Close range that
can be easily climbed. Its height can grow up to Far range.

**Mark a Stress** to use this spell as an attack. Make a **Spellcast Roll**
against an adversary or group of adversaries within Close range. The erupting
stalk lifts targets you succeed against into the air and drops them, dealing
**d8** physical damage using your Proficiency.
