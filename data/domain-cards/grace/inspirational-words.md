## INSPIRATIONAL WORDS

**Level 1 Grace Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Your speech is imbued with power. After a long rest, place a number of tokens on
this card equal to your Presence. When you speak with an ally, you can spend a
token from this card to give them one benefit from the following options:

- Your ally clears a Stress. 
- Your ally clears a Hit Point. 
- Your ally gains a Hope.

When you take a long rest, clear all unspent tokens.
