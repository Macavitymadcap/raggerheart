## WHIRLWIND

**Level 1 Blade Ability**

**Recall Cost: 0**

When you make a successful attack against a target within Very Close range, you
can **spend a Hope** to use the attack against all other targets within Very
Close range. All additional adversaries you succeed against with this ability
take half damage.
