### HEAD VAMPIRE

***Tier 3 Leader***

_A captivating undead dressed in aristocratic finery._

**Motives & Tactics:** Create thralls, charm, command, fly, intimidate

**Difficulty:** 17 | **Thresholds:** 22/42 | **HP:** 6 | **Stress:** 6

**ATK:** +5 | **Rapier:** Melee | 2d20+4 phy

**Experience:** Aristocrat +3

**FEATURES**

***Terrifying - Passive:*** When the Vampire makes a successful attack, all PCs
within Far range lose a Hope and you gain a Fear.

***Look into My Eyes - Passive:*** A creature who moves into Melee range of the
Vampire must make an Instinct Reaction Roll. On a failure, you gain **1d4**
Fear.

***Feed on Followers - Action:*** When the Vampire is within Melee range of an
ally, they can cause the ally to mark a HP. The Vampire then clears a HP.

***The Hunt Is On - Action:*** **Spend 2 Fear** to summon **1d4** Vampires, who
appear at Far range and immediately take the spotlight.

***Lifesuck - Reaction:*** When the Vampire is spotlighted, roll a **d8**. On a
result of 6 or higher, all targets within Very Close range must mark a HP.
