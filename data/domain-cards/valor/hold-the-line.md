## HOLD THE LINE

**Level 9 Valor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Describe the defensive stance you take and **spend a Hope**. If an adversary
moves within Very Close range, they're pulled into Melee range and _Restrained_.

This condition lasts until you move or fail a roll with Fear, or the GM spends 2
Fear on their turn to clear it.
