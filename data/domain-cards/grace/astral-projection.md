## ASTRAL PROJECTION

**Level 8 Grace Spell**

**Recall Cost: 0**

Once per long rest, **mark a Stress** to create a projected copy of yourself
that can appear anywhere you've been before.

You can see and hear through the projection as though it were you and affect the
world as though you were there. A creature investigating the projection can tell
it's of magical origin. This effect lasts until your next rest or your
projection takes any damage.
