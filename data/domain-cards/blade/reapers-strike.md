## REAPER'S STRIKE

**Level 9 Blade Ability**

**Recall Cost: 3**

Once per long rest, **spend a Hope** to make an attack roll. The GM tells you
which targets within range it would succeed against. Choose one of these targets
and force them to mark 5 Hit Points.
