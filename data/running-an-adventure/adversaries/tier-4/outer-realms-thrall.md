### OUTER REALMS THRALL

***Tier 4 Minion***

_A vaguely humanoid form stripped of memory and identity._

**Motives & Tactics:** Destroy, disgust, disorient, intimidate

**Difficulty:** 17 | **Thresholds:** None | **HP:** 1 | **Stress:** 1

**ATK:** +3 | **Claws and Teeth:** Very Close | 11 phy

**FEATURES**

***Minion (13) - Passive:*** The Thrall is defeated when they take any damage.
For every 13 damage a PC deals to the Thrall, defeat an additional Minion within
range the attack would succeed against.

***Group Attack - Action:*** **Spend a Fear** to choose a target and spotlight
all Outer Realm Thralls within Close range of them. Those Minions move into
Melee range of the target and make one shared attack roll. On a success, they
deal 11 physical damage each. Combine this damage.
