## INVIGORATION

**Level 10 Splendor Spell**

**Recall Cost: 3**

When you or an ally within Close range has used a feature that has an exhaustion
limit (such as once per rest or once per session), you can **spend any number of**
**Hope** and roll that many **d6s**. If any roll a 6, the feature can be used
again.
