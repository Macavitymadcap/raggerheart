## VICIOUS ENTANGLE

**Level 1 Sage Spell**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Make a **Spellcast Roll** against a target within Far range. On a success, roots
and vines reach out from the ground, dealing **1d8+1** physical damage and
temporarily _Restraining_ the target.

Additionally on a success, you can **spend a Hope** to temporarily _Restrain_
another adversary within Very Close range of your target.
