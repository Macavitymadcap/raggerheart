### GIANT BEASTMASTER

***Tier 2 Leader***

_A leather-clad warrior bearing a whip and massive bow._

**Motives & Tactics:** Command, make a living, maneuver, pin down, protect
companion animals

**Difficulty:** 16 | **Thresholds:** 12/24 | **HP:** 6 | **Stress:** 5

**ATK:** +2 | **Longbow:** Far | 2d8+4 phy

**Experience:** Animal Handling +3

**FEATURES**

***Two as One - Passive:*** When the Beastmaster is spotlighted, you can also
spotlight a Tier 1 animal adversary currently under their control.

***Pinning Strike - Action:*** Make a standard attack against a target. On a
success, you can **mark a Stress** to pin them to a nearby surface. The pinned
target is _Restrained_ until they break free with a successful Finesse or
Strength Roll.

***Deadly Companion - Action:*** Twice per scene, summon a Bear, Dire Wolf, or
similar Tier 1 animal adversary under the Beastmaster's control. The adversary
appears at Close range and is immediately spotlighted.
