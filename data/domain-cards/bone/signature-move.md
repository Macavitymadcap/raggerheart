## SIGNATURE MOVE

**Level 5 Bone Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

Name and describe your signature combat move. Once per rest, when you perform
this signature move as part of an action you're taking, you can roll a **d20**
as your Hope Die. On a success, clear a Stress.
