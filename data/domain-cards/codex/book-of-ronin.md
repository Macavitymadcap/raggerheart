## BOOK OF RONIN

**Level 9 Codex Grimoire**

**Recall Cost: 4**

***Transform:*** Make a **Spellcast Roll (15)**. On a success, transform into an
inanimate object no larger than twice your normal size. You can remain in this
shape until you take damage.

***Eternal Enervation:*** Once per long rest, make a **Spellcast Roll** against
a target within Close range. On a success, they become permanently _Vulnerable_.
They can't clear this condition by any means.
