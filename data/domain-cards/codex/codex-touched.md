## CODEX-TOUCHED

**Level 7 Codex Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

When 4 or more of the domain cards in your loadout are from the Codex domain,
gain the following benefits:

- You can **mark a Stress** to add your Proficiency to a Spellcast Roll. 
- Once per rest, replace this card with any card from your vault without paying itsRecall Cost.
