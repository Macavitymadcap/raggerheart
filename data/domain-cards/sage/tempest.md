## TEMPEST

**Level 10 Sage Spell**

**Recall Cost: 2**

Choose one of the following tempests and make a **Spellcast Roll** against all
targets within Far range. Targets you succeed against experience its effects
until the GM spends a Fear on their turn to end this spell.

- ***Blizzard***: Deal **2d20+8** magic damage and targets are temporarily
  _Vulnerable_. 
- ***Hurricane***: Deal **3d10+10** magic damage and choose a
  direction the wind is blowing. Targets can't move against the wind. 
- ***Sandstorm***: Deal **5d6+9** magic damage. Attacks made from beyond Melee
  range have disadvantage.
