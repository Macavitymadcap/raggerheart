### MINOR TREANT

***Tier 1 Minion***

_An ambulatory sapling rising up to defend their forest._

**Motives & Tactics:** Crush, overwhelm, protect

**Difficulty:** 10 | **Thresholds:** None | **HP:** 1 | **Stress:** 1

**ATK:** -2 | **Clawed Branch:** Melee | 4 phy

**FEATURES**

***Minion (5) - Passive:*** The Treant is defeated when they take any damage.
For every 5 damage a PC deals to the Treant, defeat an additional Minion within
range the attack would succeed against.

***Group Attack - Action:*** **Spend a Fear** to choose a target and spotlight
all Minor Treants within Close range of them. Those Minions move into Melee
range of the target and make one shared attack roll. On a success, they deal 4
physical damage each. Combine this damage.
