## SOOTHING SPEECH

**Level 4 Grace Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

During a short rest, when you take the time to comfort another character while
using the Tend to Wounds downtime move on them, clear an additional Hit Point on
that character. When you do, you also clear 2 Hit Points.
