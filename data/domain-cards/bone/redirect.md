## REDIRECT

**Level 4 Bone Ability**

**Recall Cost: 1**

When an attack made against you from beyond Melee range fails, roll a number of
**d6s** equal to your Proficiency. If any roll a 6, you can **mark a Stress** to
redirect the attack to damage an adversary within Very Close range instead.
