## MASS DISGUISE

**Level 6 Midnight Spell**

**Recall Cost: 0**

When you have a few minutes of silence to focus, you can **mark a Stress** to
change the appearance of all willing creatures within Close range. Their new
forms must share a general body structure and size, and can be somebody or
something you've seen before or entirely fabricated. A disguised creature has
advantage on Presence Rolls to avoid scrutiny.

Activate a Countdown (8). It ticks down as a consequence the GM chooses. When it
triggers, the disguise drops.
