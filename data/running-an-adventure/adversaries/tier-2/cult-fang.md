### CULT FANG

***Tier 2 Skulk***

_A professional killer-turned-cultist._

**Motives & Tactics:** Capture sacrifices, isolate prey, rise in the ranks

**Difficulty:** 15 | **Thresholds:** 9/17 | **HP:** 4 | **Stress:** 4

**ATK:** +2 | **Long Knife:** Melee | 2d8+4 phy

**FEATURES**

***Shadow's Embrace - Passive:*** The Fang can climb and walk on vertical
surfaces. **Mark a Stress** to move from one shadow to another within Far range.

***Pick Off the Straggler - Action:*** **Mark a Stress** to cause a target
within Melee range to make an Instinct Reaction Roll. On a failure, the target
must mark 2 Stress and is teleported with the Fang to a shadow within Far range,
making them temporarily _Vulnerable_. On a success, the target must mark a
Stress.
