### YOUNG DRYAD

***Tier 1 Leader***

_An imperious tree-person leading their forest's defenses._

**Motives & Tactics:** Command, nurture, prune the unwelcome

**Difficulty:** 11 | **Thresholds:** 6/11 | **HP:** 6 | **Stress:** 2

**ATK:** +0 | **Scythe:** Melee | 1d8+5 phy

**Experience:** Leadership +3

**FEATURES**

***Voice of the Forest - Action:*** **Mark a Stress** to spotlight **1d4**
allies within range of a target they can attack without moving. On a success,
their attacks deal half damage.

***Thorny Cage - Action:*** **Spend a Fear** to form a cage around a target
within Very Close range and _Restrain_ them until they're freed with a
successful Strength Roll. When a creature makes an action roll against the cage,
they must mark a Stress.

***Momentum - Reaction:*** When the Dryad makes a successful attack against a
PC, you gain a Fear.
