## COPYCAT

**Level 9 Grace Spell**

**Recall Cost: 3**

Once per long rest, this card can mimic the features of another domain card of
level 8 or lower in another player's loadout. **Spend Hope equal to half the
card's level** to gain access to the feature. It lasts until your next rest or
they place the card in their vault.
