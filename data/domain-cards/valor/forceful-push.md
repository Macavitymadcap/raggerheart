## FORCEFUL PUSH

**Level 1 Valor Ability**

**Recall Cost: 0**

Make an attack with your primary weapon against a target within Melee range. On
a success, you deal damage and knock them back to Close range. On a success with
Hope, add a **d6** to your damage roll.

Additionally, you can **spend a Hope** to make them temporarily _Vulnerable_.
