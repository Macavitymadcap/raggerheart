## STEALTH EXPERTISE

**Level 4 Midnight Ability**

**Recall Cost: 0**

When you roll with Fear while attempting to move unnoticed through a dangerous
area, you can **mark a Stress** to roll with Hope instead.

If an ally within Close range is also attempting to move unnoticed and rolls
with Fear, you can **mark a Stress** to change their result to a roll with Hope.
