## BATTLE CRY

**Level 8 Blade Ability**

**Recall Cost: 2**

Once per long rest, while you're charging into danger, you can muster a rousing
call that inspires your allies. All allies who can hear you each clear a Stress
and gain a Hope. Additionally, your allies gain advantage on attack rolls until
you or an ally rolls a failure with Fear.
